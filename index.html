Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer custom furniture design? +
Yes! We specialize in custom furniture design tailored to your specific requirements, space, and style preferences. Our design team works closely with you to create unique pieces.
What is your delivery timeline? +
Standard products are typically delivered within 2-3 weeks. Custom furniture may take 4-6 weeks depending on the complexity of the design. We always provide accurate timelines during consultation.
Do you provide installation services? +
Absolutely! All our furniture comes with professional installation services. Our skilled technicians ensure perfect setup and finishing at your location.
What warranty do you offer? +
We provide a comprehensive warranty ranging from 1-5 years depending on the product category. This covers manufacturing defects and structural issues.
What payment options are available? +
We accept all major payment methods including cash, card, online transfers, and EMI options. Flexible payment plans are available for larger orders.
Can I see samples before ordering? +
Yes! Visit our showroom to see our products and material samples. We can also arrange home visits with sample materials for your convenience.
